99 · Negative & edge states (reference)

This is the negative-states reference board — a flex-wrap of composed snippets, not a device screen. It collects the ten failure and edge cases the app must handle gracefully and shows the exact components each uses, so every live screen treats them consistently. Key intent: failures are never dead ends — every panel preserves context and gives one clear way forward (Retry, Resend, Clear filters, Sign in, Update payment, queue offline, or re-open a thread). Missing media falls back to a dimensioned .media__ph or initials avatar; broken/upload errors keep the slot and a Retry; validation flags the field and disables the primary action without hiding it; expired sessions preserve drafts; offline disables net-bound actions and queues them; and a rejected applicant gets "View your application" + message instead of a duplicate apply.
