95 · Create menu — the + button (worker)

The definitive answer to "what does the + do." Tapping the center FAB dims the feed behind a .scrim, rotates the + 45° into a close affordance (.anim-fab-open), and floats a .createmenu of context actions just above it that rise in sequence (.anim-stagger). Worker options: New post (i-edit), List equipment (i-truck, into Marketplace), and Share a job (i-share). Tapping the scrim or the rotated FAB dismisses with no change. The + is role-adaptive like the rest of the shell: in Employer mode the dark FAB expands to Post a job (the primary, top), New post, List equipment, and Reach out (start a candidate outreach) — same component, employer-weighted ordering. This keeps "create" a single predictable gesture across both modes while surfacing the actions that matter for the active role.

Flows: create action (POST-01 / EQ-01 / JOB-05) · Depends on: tabbar__fab, scrim, createmenu (+ actionsheet__icon/sub), anim-scrim, anim-fab-open, anim-stagger, press · Mode: worker (employer variant noted)
