14 · Pick your trades (worker onboarding)

Step 3 of worker onboarding. The screen scales to the full M2 taxonomy: a curated "Most popular" chip set (HVAC, Electrical, Plumbing, Welding, Carpentry, Heavy Equipment, General Labor, Masonry, Roofing) sits above a "Show all trades" expander and a searchbar that reaches all 19 categories. Selected trades become removable active chips ("Your trades"), and the worker can pick MULTIPLE — an "Add another trade" button makes that explicit. Once a trade is chosen, the same popular-chips + search + "All specialties" pattern lets them narrow to sub-trades (e.g. Residential HVAC, Sheet Metal / Ductwork). Experience uses the 8-level platform standard (Helper→Project Manager), surfacing the five most common levels with the rest behind "More levels". IronLink pre-checks HVAC and Electrical (and pre-selects Journeyman + Residential HVAC) from role hints captured earlier in signup, so most users confirm rather than fill a blank form — important for lower-literacy users. The action stays docked so Continue is always reachable; no tab bar appears during onboarding.
