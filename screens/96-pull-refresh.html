96 · Pull to refresh (feed)

Captures the live moment after a refresh gesture on the worker Network feed. As the user drags down, the feed translates downward to expose the .ptr affordance; once the drag passes the trigger threshold (~60px) the spinner enters its .is-ready state (yellow tip, slight scale-up). On release the request fires and the spinner switches to .is-spinning — a continuous il-spin at 0.8s linear with the "Refreshing…" label — while the held offset (60px) keeps the content parked below. When fresh data lands the offset releases and the cards settle back up, re-entering via .anim-stagger (il-rise, 320ms cubic-bezier(.4,0,.2,1), 40ms stepped delays per card) so the list feels physically restacked rather than swapped. Under prefers-reduced-motion the spin and rise are suppressed (CSS guard), leaving a static spinner and an instant content swap. Offline, the gesture is allowed but resolves to the cached feed with a synced-time hint instead of new posts.
