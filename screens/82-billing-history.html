The employer's billing home. The top highlight card states the live subscription at a glance — Professional, $149/mo, renews Jul 1 — and offers the two paths an employer reaches for here: "Manage plan" (→ 80) to change tier and "Payment method" (→ 83) to update the card on file. Below, a flush invoice list pairs each date + line-item with its amount in .tabnum and a status tag — Paid uses tag--pos, the still-settling Featured-post charge uses tag--warn Pending. A per-row i-download pulls the PDF receipt; the Pending row's download is dimmed (is-stale) and disabled until the charge clears. Key UX decision: money stays right-aligned and tabular so amounts scan vertically, and status is a single glanceable tag rather than prose.
